Stranger Things

2016 TV-14 4 Seasons

When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one strange little girl.

About

Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the series centers around the disappearance of Will Byers and the subsequent appearance of a psychokinetic girl who helps his friends in their search.

The first season begins in November 1983, when Will Byers is abducted by a creature from the Upside Down. His mother, Joyce, and the town's police chief, Jim Hopper, search for Will. At the same time, a young psychokinetic girl escapes from the laboratory and helps Will's friends in their own efforts to find Will.

Cast: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder

Genres: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama

Maturity Rating: TV-14
